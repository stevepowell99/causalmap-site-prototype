You can access Causal Map for <span class="hl hl-green">free</span> by signing up in the app. No credit card required.

The core functionality is free for everyone: unlimited projects, unlimited coding.

Choose your subscription directly from the Account tab in the app: [app.causalmap.app](https://app.causalmap.app/)

**Free:** Everything you need to get started. Unlimited projects and coding. Your projects are public.

**Private** (from £18/month): Keep your projects private. Email support.

**Pro** (from £38/month): Everything in Private, plus [versioning](https://garden.causalmap.app/versioning/) (continuous backups), [bookmarks and reports](https://garden.causalmap.app/bookmarks-reports/), and a dedicated [statistics panel](https://garden.causalmap.app/pivot-panel/).

**Team** (from £78/month per seat): Everything in Pro, plus [sharing between team members](https://garden.causalmap.app/sharing-and-permissions/), [live collaboration](https://garden.causalmap.app/live-collaboration/), [logs of all edits](https://garden.causalmap.app/logs-panel/), and two-factor authentication. [Contact us](mailto:hello@causalmap.app) for team discounts.

## AI add-on

Any paid subscription can add <span class="hl hl-yellow">AI features</span> for an additional £18/month (£144/year). Free users get a fixed monthly allowance of AI credits at no cost.

With the AI add-on you get:

- [Simple AI Coding](https://garden.causalmap.app/simple-ai/): one-click AI coding for fast causal link extraction.
- [AI Answers](https://garden.causalmap.app/answers-panel/): ask natural language questions about your data and get AI-generated answers with supporting quotes.
- [Vignettes](https://garden.causalmap.app/howto-vignettes/): generate narrative summaries of your causal maps.
- [Soft Recode Plus](https://garden.causalmap.app/filter-link-tab/#soft-recode-plus): group messy labels under clearer names using AI suggestions.
- [Auto Recode](https://garden.causalmap.app/filter-link-tab/#hierarchical-cluster-filter): turn labels into a hierarchical tree for easy exploration.

### More about subscriptions

Any user, including free users, can view (but not edit) any project that is public or shared with them by its creator. Free users' projects are all public.

Live support sessions are only available for Team subscriptions. Other users can use [The Guide](https://garden.causalmap.app/) and [tutorial videos](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLSCKdSxlLlfGfcab5njcT57xzU0hOURc-&si=jX6S7fu43kBzHnRc). Please report bugs via the Report a Bug function in the app.

To pause or cancel your subscription, email [hello@causalmap.app](mailto:hello@causalmap.app) or [gabriele@causalmap.app](mailto:gabriele@causalmap.app).
