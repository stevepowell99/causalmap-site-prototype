## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Effective date: 1 January 2024**

### 1. Availability guarantee

We aim to maintain <span class="hl hl-green">99.5% uptime</span> for the Causal Map Service. Uptime is measured excluding scheduled maintenance and events beyond our control (e.g. ISP outages, DDoS attacks).

### 2. Scheduled maintenance

We perform maintenance during off-peak hours (typically 00:00-06:00 UTC, weekdays). We will notify users at least 48 hours in advance of significant maintenance.

### 3. Support response times

**Free and Private plans:**
- Email support: response within 48 hours on business days

**Pro and Team plans:**
- Priority email support: <span class="hl hl-yellow">response within 24 hours</span> on business days
- Dedicated support contact for Team plans
- Phone support available for Team plan customers

### 4. Performance targets

- Page load time: <3 seconds on typical connections
- Data export: Complete within 5 minutes for projects under 50,000 links
- API response time: <1 second for 95th percentile of requests

### 5. Data backup

- Your data is <span class="hl hl-pink">backed up daily</span> and retained for 30 days.
- You can restore your data via your account or contact support.

### 6. Security

- All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).
- We conduct annual security audits and penetration tests.
- We comply with GDPR, UK Data Protection Act 2018, and ISO 27001 principles.

### 7. Incident response

If the Service experiences an outage:
- We will investigate immediately and post status updates on our status page.
- We aim to restore service within 4 hours for critical incidents.
- We will notify customers of incidents and resolution timelines.

### 8. Service credits

If we fail to meet our 99.5% uptime target in a calendar month, qualifying customers are entitled to service credits:

- 99%-99.5% uptime: 5% credit
- 95%-99% uptime: 10% credit
- <95% uptime: 25% credit

Credits are applied to the next billing period. Credits do not constitute a refund of fees.

To claim a credit, contact [hello@causalmap.app](mailto:hello@causalmap.app) within 30 days of the incident.

### 9. Exclusions

Service credits do not apply to:

- Downtime caused by events outside our control (natural disasters, ISP issues, cyberattacks).
- Downtime resulting from your use of the Service in violation of our terms.
- Issues caused by third-party services or plugins you use with the Service.
- Scheduled maintenance of which we gave notice.

### 10. Changes to SLA

We may update this SLA with 30 days notice. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance.

### 11. Contact

For SLA questions or to report incidents, contact [hello@causalmap.app](mailto:hello@causalmap.app) or check our status page.
